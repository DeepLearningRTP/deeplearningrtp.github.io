About

A study group, not a lecture series.

Deep Learning RTP is for people who want to understand and build neural networks. Each weekly session is a paper review or a technical deep dive. Between sessions, work through a course or project at your own pace — then come to ask questions, answer them, or present what you've figured out.

All experience levels and professional backgrounds are welcome.

Code of conduct

Be kind. Be open.

We follow the spirit of the Recurse Center social rules. Harassment isn't tolerated; organizers may take any action they deem appropriate. Deep Learning RTP is a fully open, public forum — assume no privacy, and please don't share confidential, proprietary, or sensitive material. The group isn't liable for content shared by individual members, visitors, or speakers.
