About

A study group, not a lecture series.

Deep Learning RTP is for people who want to understand and build neural networks.

Each weekly session is a paper review or a technical deep dive. Between sessions, work through a course or project at your own pace — then come to ask questions, answer them, or present what you've figured out. All experience levels and professional backgrounds are welcome.

Code of conduct

Deep Learning RTP is a fully open, public forum — assume no privacy, and please don't share confidential, proprietary, or sensitive material.
