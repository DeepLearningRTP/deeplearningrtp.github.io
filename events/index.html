DLRTP Weekly — Emergent Sparsity in Frozen-Random-CNN Deep RL (Scott Norton)

Wed Jul 22, 2026 · 12:00 PM EDT · Frontier RTP, 800 Park Offices Dr, Durham, NC

Playing Pong through 3 neurons: freeze a randomly initialized CNN, train a deep RL agent on top, and the agent spontaneously compresses all task-relevant information through as few as 1-3 of the 64 neurons in its first fully-connected layer, with no sparsity objective anywhere. Scott Norton presents his manuscript (arXiv pending) documenting the phenomenon in Atari Pong. No RL background required; the setting is informal and open to everyone. Find us in the Eagles room off the main coworking area.
